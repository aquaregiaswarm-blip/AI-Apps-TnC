## Critical AI Data Practices

### Q: How does Notion AI use my data?

**Answer:**
> "We follow our standard data protection practices to keep your data encrypted and private. **Notion AI will not use your data to train our models unless you opt in to a request to share your data.**

> Any information used to power Notion AI will be shared with AI subprocessors for the sole purpose of providing you with the Notion AI features. **We specifically have contractual agreements in place with our AI subprocessors that prohibit the use of customer data to train their models.**"

**Key Points:**
- ✅ No AI training without explicit opt-in
- ✅ Subprocessor contracts prohibit training on customer data
- ✅ Data encrypted and private
- ✅ Shared only for providing AI features
