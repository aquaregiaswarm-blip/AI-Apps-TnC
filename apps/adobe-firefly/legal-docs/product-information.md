## What is Adobe Firefly?

Family of creative generative AI models embedded in Adobe's flagship apps and Adobe Stock.

## Training Data

**Commercially Safe Approach:**
- ✅ Licensed images from Adobe Stock
- ✅ Public domain content (copyright expired)
- ❌ Does NOT train on Creative Cloud subscribers' personal content
- ❌ Does NOT train on Adobe Stock editorial content

## Key Features

### Content Credentials
- Automatic digital "nutrition label"
- Shows AI was used in creation
- C2PA industry standard
- Part of Content Authenticity Initiative (3,300+ members)

### Languages
- 100+ languages for text prompts
- 20+ languages for audio/video translation

### Integration
- Embedded in Creative Cloud apps
- Photoshop, Illustrator, Express, etc.
- Adobe Stock integration

## Generative Credits

- Free: 25 credits/month
- Premium: 100 credits/month
- Included with Creative Cloud subscriptions
- Additional credits available for purchase

## Custom Models

Enterprise feature allowing organizations to:
- Train on their own brand assets
- Generate content with company styles
- Maintain brand consistency

## Firefly Services

API for enterprise integration:
- Content generation at scale
- Workflow automation
- Integration with enterprise systems
- https://developer.adobe.com/firefly-services/
