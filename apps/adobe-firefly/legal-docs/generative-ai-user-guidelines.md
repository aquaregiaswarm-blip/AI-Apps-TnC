**Last Updated:** April 24, 2025

## Key Sections

### 1. What is Adobe Firefly?

Adobe Firefly is a family of creative generative AI models embedded in Adobe's flagship apps and Adobe Stock.

### 2. Training Data - Commercially Safe

**Critical Point:**
> "As part of our work to design Firefly to be commercially safe, we trained our first Firefly model on licensed images from Adobe Stock and public domain content where copyright has expired."

**What Firefly Does NOT Train On:**
- ❌ Creative Cloud subscribers' personal content
- ❌ Adobe Stock editorial content
- ❌ User-uploaded content (without explicit opt-in)

### 3. Content Credentials

Adobe automatically attaches **Content Credentials** to images created in Firefly:
- Digital "nutrition label" for content
- Shows how and when content was created
- Indicates AI was used
- Part of C2PA industry standard
- Cross-industry coalition (CAI - Content Authenticity Initiative)

### 4. Usage Rights

**Commercial Use:**
- ✅ Outputs from non-beta features: **Can be used commercially**
- ⚠️ Outputs from beta features: **Personal use only** (unless otherwise designated)
- ⚠️ Beta outputs: **Not eligible for indemnification** while in beta

### 5. User Responsibilities

Users must NOT:
- Remove or alter Content Credentials
- Use outputs for illegal purposes
- Create harmful, deceptive, or misleading content
- Violate third-party rights

### 6. IP Indemnification

**Available for:**
- Premium/Enterprise tiers
- Non-beta features

**Not Available for:**
- Free tier
- Beta features

### 7. Account Requirements

- Requires Adobe account
- Subject to Adobe General Terms of Use
- Generative AI features may have additional terms

> "We do not train on any Creative Cloud subscribers' personal content."

Beta outputs are personal use only and not indemnified
