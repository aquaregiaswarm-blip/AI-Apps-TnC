## Critical Section: AI Training

**Section: Use of Customer Data for AI Training**

> "Relume may use certain customer data to improve our services through AI training and model development. This data usage is aimed at enhancing the features and functionality of our services, including providing more accurate and personalized experiences for our users. When we use customer data for AI training, we prioritize data privacy and protection by anonymizing or aggregating data where feasible. Your personal information will not be disclosed or sold to third parties for their independent use."

**Key Points:**
- Relume trains AI on customer data
- Used for "enhancing features and functionality"
- Data is anonymized/aggregated where feasible
- Personal information not sold to third parties

**CRITICAL - Opt-Out Available:**
> "If you have questions or concerns regarding the use of your data for AI training, please contact us at [hello@relume.io. **You can opt out in account settings.**]"

## Information Collection

**Section: Gathering of Information**

Relume collects:
- Name, email address, passwords, security data
- Payment information
- Browser type, language preference, referring site
- Date and time of visitor requests
- Cookies and usage data

**Section: Website Visitors**
> "Relume automatically collects non-personally-identifying information of the sort that web browsers and servers typically make available, such as the browser type, language preference, referring site, and the date and time of each visitor request."

---

## Data Protection

**Section: Protection of Information**

> "Relume discloses potentially personally-identifying and personally-identifying information only to those of its employees, contractors and affiliated organisations that (i) need to know that information in order to process it on Relume's behalf or to provide services available at Relume's website, and (ii) that have agreed not to disclose it to others."

**International Transfer:**
> "Some of those employees, contractors and affiliated organisations may be located outside of your home country; by using Relume's website, you consent to the transfer of such information to them."

## Security

**Section: Security**

> "The security of your Personal Information is important to us, but remember that no method of transmission over the Internet, or method of electronic storage is 100% secure. While we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your Personal Information, we cannot guarantee its absolute security."
