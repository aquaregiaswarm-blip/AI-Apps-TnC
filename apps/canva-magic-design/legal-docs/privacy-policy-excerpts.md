# Canva Privacy Policy - Key Excerpts

**Source:** https://www.canva.com/policies/privacy-policy/  
**Accessed:** March 26, 2026  
**Last Updated:** March 16, 2026

## Critical Section: AI Training

**Section 2 - How we use your information:**

> "We may analyze your activity, content, media uploads and related data in your account to provide and customize the Service, and to **train our algorithms, models and AI products and services using machine learning** to develop, improve and provide our Service."

**Key Points:**
- Canva trains AI on user content
- Includes "activity, content, media uploads"
- Used for "algorithms, models and AI products"
- Applies to Magic Design and other AI features

---

## Information Collection

**Section 1 - Information we collect:**

### (a) Information you provide directly
- Username, names, birthdate, phone, profession, address
- Messages, feedback, search queries, **prompts**
- **User Content: designs, images, documents, videos**

### (j) Content within your account
> "We receive content that you create within Canva and media you upload, such as designs, images, documents, videos, and metadata about your content."

### (d) Information collected automatically
- User activity data
- Analytics event data
- Clickstream data
- **Used for data analytics and machine learning**

---

## Data Usage for AI

**Section 2 - Service improvement:**
> "For Service improvement (including analytics and machine learning): We may analyze your activity, content, media uploads and related data in your account to provide and customize the Service, and to train our algorithms, models and AI products and services using machine learning to develop, improve and provide our Service."

**You can manage:**
- Privacy preferences at https://www.canva.com/settings/privacy-preferences
- Opt out of data collection from third-party providers
- Cookie preferences

---

## Third-Party Data

**Section 1(c) - Information from other third parties:**
- Public sources
- Social media platforms
- Third-party data providers
- Used for "personalized offers and services"

---

## Important Notes

### ⚠️ AI Training Confirmed
Canva explicitly states in Privacy Policy that user content is used to train AI models. This includes:
- Designs created in Canva
- Images uploaded
- Documents and videos
- Prompts entered
- Activity and behavior

### ⚠️ Opt-Out Unclear
Privacy Policy mentions managing preferences but does not clearly state if users can opt out of AI training specifically.

### ⚠️ No Retention Specified
Privacy Policy does not specify how long data is retained after account deletion.
