# Galileo AI Pricing Information

**Source:** https://galileo.ai/pricing  
**Accessed:** March 26, 2026

---

## Pricing Tiers

### Free
**Price:** $0/month

**Includes:**
- 5,000 traces per month
- Unlimited users
- Unlimited custom evals

**Limitations:**
- No SSO/RBAC
- No dedicated support
- Shared infrastructure
- No SLA

---

### Pro
**Price:** $100/month (billed yearly, save 33%)

**Includes:**
- 50,000 traces per month
- Standard RBAC
- Advanced analytics & insights
- Dedicated support: Slack

**Note:** Pricing scales based on number of traces

---

### Enterprise
**Price:** Contact us (custom)

**Includes:**
- Unlimited traces
- Custom rate limits
- **Deploy options: Hosted, VPC, or on-prem**
- Enterprise-grade security RBAC, SSO
- Dedicated CSM
- Real-time guardrails
- 24/7 Support: Slack, email, or phone
- Low-latency dedicated inference servers
- Forward deployed engineering support
